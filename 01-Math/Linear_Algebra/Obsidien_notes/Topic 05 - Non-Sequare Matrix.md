## 📌 Summary
A non-square matrix (e.g. 2×3) transforms vectors between spaces of different dimensions.

---

## 1. Non-Square Matrix

Defined as a **2×3 matrix**, where the 3 vectors are the basis vectors $\hat{i}$, $\hat{j}$, and $\hat{k}$ — meaning we are in **three-dimensional** space, and the two rows mean the output is in **two-dimensional** space.

- 3 columns → input is 3D
- 2 rows → output is 2D
- The landing spots mean the **output is in two-dimensional space**
